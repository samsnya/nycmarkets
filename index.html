Markets

NEW YORK CITY

New Yorkers are savvy bargain hunters seeking variety, collectables, negotiable prices, and handmade crafts.

So where would New Yorkers go you say?!! To the various Markets of New York City!

Here you will find popular flea markets that are similar to giant garage sales, food markets offering delicacies found around the world, and artisan markets displaying antiques and handcrafts made by distinguished craftsmen.
